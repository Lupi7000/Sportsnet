30 Thoughts: Buyers and Sellers at the Ithaca Hockey Fantasy Trade Deadline

Chris Johnston explains why the Vegas Golden Knights could get involved with the NHL trade deadline and talks about the battered and bruised Ottawa Senators.

Elliotte Friedman February 28, 2017, 3:02 PM
Elliotte Friedman February 28, 2017, 3:02 PM

• Sheriff Gene Freak calls own team "farts"
• Can JackD Off W/ Girgens cash in?
• How did Jamie end up getting me, Elliotte Friedman, to write about this small fantasy hockey league?

We’re under 100 hours from the Ithaca Hockey trade deadline (depending on when perennial cellar dweller and commissioner Matt Lupica gets his shit together). Somehow, someway, JoePCaruso sits in first place. Let’s jump right in with our pre-deadline 30 Thoughts.

30 THOUGHTS

1.Folks, The Pavel Gadzyuk’s are a good team. You don’t want to admit it, and I don’t want to admit it, but it’s real, and it’s happening. This is a balanced lineup, but most importantly, it has a number of forwards with dual eligibility to maximize its output. They’re in the top half of the league in all offensive categories. The goaltending has been a bit suspect of late, but they’re a contender. The only question is – will their GM somehow screw this up?

2. I do believe this title is completely up for grabs. All six teams that are currently in the playoff picture (and that’s how it looks it will finish up) could win this title. Johnson Family Trust in sixth place boasts players like Patrick Kane, Cam Atkinson, and Braden Holtby.

3. Building off of that point, the teams that are currently in the Top 6 are the six teams we will see in the playoffs – the Top 4 are already clinched. I don’t see a run coming from Benn There Doan That, despite talent on their roster. Let’s give credit where credit is due to perennial cellar-dweller RIP Gordie Howerambe. They went 2-0-2 heading into last week, but saw their Cinderella run end with a crushing loss to JFT last week. Top 6, take notice - plenty of talent on that roster though. Expect Matt to do what he does best over the next few days – sell, sell, sell. Also, love me some sibling on sibling twitter violence.

4. Speaking of The Pavel Gadzyuk’s – can anyone tell me why this team has an apostrophe in its name?

5. The top d-man on the move is Benn There Doan That’s Duncan Keith. His keeper contract expires this year. Joey would be foolish not to move him. The question is, what’s the asking price? Brent Burns, arguably the MVP of this league, was acquired in exchange for a 1st – have to think Keith is slightly lesser than that, BUT, the defending champs grabbed him early. Near the deadline, with the league up in the air, prices could become inflated.

6. The top-ranked player in the last 30 days? Jonathan Toews. JackD Off W/ Girgens SHOULD cash in. There is a ton of talent on this roster – Crosby, Marner, Martinez, Marchand, Pastrnak – he won’t win the league, but he could determine the winner.

7. Ryan is out and selling his players, but not for lack of trying, with by far the most moves in the league this year. The acquisition of Giroux did not pan out. He needs to sell high on Filip Forsberg, his top chip to prep for 2017-18. I was surprised to see the move of Hanzal for Backstrom, on both sides of the coin. Per Commish Lupica, he wasn't sure why he made the deal either."I dunno, but I got a 5." Well, OK.

8. You know how important goal-scoring is? JackD Off W/ Girgens LEADS the league. And isn’t making the postseason.

9. Sources say that Jagr Bombs is looking to upgrade at wing and defense. Perhaps I should send you Benn There Doan That’s contact info.

10. I’m told Ovechkin, Carter, Buff, and Price are off the table there, but everyone else is fair game.

11. JoePCaruso, per usual, never wants to rest on his laurels. I believe he’s trying to acquire a D-man that can rack up the hits and blocks (paging Jack Capuano’s Hair and Radko Gudas) as well as some insurance at wing. Looking at that roster, you’d think that Galchenyuk, Werenski, and Skinner are the chips he’s trying to move.

12. Give the man credit. We all know he’s of lesser mental capacity, and his team is going to make the postseason. Raise a glass to JoePCaruso. Nostrovia!

13. Although – anyone else confused by that swap of Luongo and Miller? Didn’t make sense to me. Smacks of a lack of experience and confidence. Trigger happy?

14. There are serious doubts in the I’m Mr. Veseyks camp about winning a title this season. A source says their GM stated in a conversation that “I don’t have the (lineup) synergy to win this year.” That can’t feel good if you’re Joe Pavelski, Auston Matthews, or Connor McDavid.

15. Defending champion Jack Capuano’s Hair is coming off a big 10-1-1 week. He’s finally getting Jonathan Quick back. But with the acquisition of Ben Bishop in Los Angeles, what to make of that goaltending scenario?

16. RIP Gordie Howerambe has two of the top five players in the last month – Jamie Benn and Patrice Bergeron – and 3 in the top 13 if you include Nick Backstrom. I’m told they’re all available.

17. JackD Off W/ Girgens has 3 of the top 17.

18. Who needs hits? FA Matt Martin has the 2nd most in the last month (52), FA Marcus Foligno is 5th (47).

19. Could Jamie and JCH get something for Peter Budaj? He leads the league in shutouts. Yes, he’s moving to Tampa – but will a team that needs goaltending take a flier?

20. Brian Campbell (+14) and Nick Schmaltz (+11) are the top two players in the league in plus-minus in the last 30 days. Both are available.

21. Do you know who leads the league in blocks? If you guessed offensive-defensive powerhouse Erik Karlsson, you’d be right (166). The man does it all.

22. How defeated is Ryan? I’ve heard from sources he’s referred to his own team in conversations with other GMs as “shambles” “turd men” and “farts.”

23. BTW, this is as good a time as any to remind everyone to follow @DamienCoxHawkey heading into the deadline. Always seems to get the scoops in the Ithaca Hockey League.

24. The current best player available? Kyle Turris, 89th across all leagues.

25. OK, I’m tired. 25 thoughts is all you get. My pick to win the league this season?

26. You’ll just have to wait for the next
